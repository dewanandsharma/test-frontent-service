Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web design, software development, and digital marketing services

General Questions

What services does Ankush Infotech provide?

We offer comprehensive IT services including custom website design and development, mobile application development (Android & iOS), software development, UI/UX design, SEO services, digital marketing, e-commerce solutions, and website maintenance. We serve businesses across Mumbai, Maharashtra, and throughout India with professional freelance IT solutions tailored to your specific needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple business website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while a custom web application or e-commerce site may take 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.

What is the cost of website development?

Website costs depend on features, design complexity, and functionality. Basic business websites start from ₹15,000-₹30,000, while custom web applications range from ₹50,000-₹2,00,000+. We offer flexible pricing and payment plans. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements.

Technical Questions

What technologies do you use for development?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js, Angular.js, Node.js, PHP, Python, C#, and databases like SQL Server, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements, scalability needs, and budget.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and mobile-optimized. We follow mobile-first design principles to ensure your website looks perfect and functions smoothly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile responsiveness is also crucial for SEO rankings.

Do you provide website hosting services?

Yes, we can help you with hosting setup and management. We work with reliable hosting providers and can recommend the best hosting solution based on your website requirements. We also provide cPanel deployment, SSL certificate installation, domain registration assistance, and ongoing technical support.

SEO & Digital Marketing

What is SEO and why do I need it?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your website rank higher in Google search results, making it easier for potential customers to find you online. Good SEO increases organic traffic, improves brand visibility, and generates quality leads. We implement on-page SEO, technical SEO, content optimization, and local SEO strategies to boost your online presence.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. You can typically expect to see initial improvements in 3-6 months, with significant results appearing after 6-12 months of consistent optimization. The timeline depends on competition, current website status, and the effectiveness of implemented strategies. We provide monthly reports to track progress.

What digital marketing services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive digital marketing services including SEO, Google Ads management, Facebook and Instagram advertising, social media marketing, content marketing, email marketing campaigns, and analytics reporting. Our strategies are data-driven and focused on delivering measurable ROI for your business.

Project Management

How does the development process work?

Our process includes: 1) Initial consultation and requirement gathering, 2) Project planning and timeline estimation, 3) Design mockups and approval, 4) Development and coding, 5) Testing and quality assurance, 6) Client review and feedback, 7) Final deployment and launch, 8) Post-launch support and maintenance. We keep you involved at every stage.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. We provide different maintenance packages based on your needs, from basic support to full-service management with regular updates and improvements.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We can build your website with a user-friendly Content Management System (CMS) like WordPress or a custom admin panel that allows you to easily update text, images, and other content without technical knowledge. We also provide training and documentation to help you manage your website independently.

Payment & Contracts

What are your payment terms?

We typically work with a milestone-based payment structure: 30% advance payment to start the project, 40% upon design approval and development completion, and 30% upon final delivery and launch. For larger projects, we can customize payment schedules. We accept bank transfers, UPI, and online payments.

Do you sign contracts or agreements?

Yes, we provide detailed project agreements that outline scope of work, deliverables, timelines, payment terms, intellectual property rights, and confidentiality clauses. This protects both parties and ensures clear expectations. We believe in transparent, professional business relationships.

Security & Maintenance

How do you ensure website security?

We implement multiple security measures including SSL certificates for encrypted connections, secure coding practices, regular security updates, firewall protection, malware scanning, secure database configurations, and backup systems. We also provide guidance on maintaining security through strong passwords and access controls.

What happens if my website goes down?

We provide emergency support for critical issues. Our maintenance packages include regular backups, uptime monitoring, and quick response times for technical problems. In case of downtime, we work immediately to diagnose and resolve the issue, restore from backups if needed, and implement measures to prevent future occurrences.
